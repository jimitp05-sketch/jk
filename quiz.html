🫀 ECMO Facts

  • → ECMO was first used successfully in 1971 by Dr. Robert Bartlett
  • → A VV-ECMO circuit processes 5–7 litres of blood per minute outside the body
  • → ECMO centre volume directly correlates with survival — high-volume centres see 55–70% survival
  • → Only ~400 centres globally are certified ELSO ECMO centres

🦠 Sepsis Facts

  • → The Sepsis-3 definition (2016) replaced SIRS criteria — qSOFA is now the screening tool
  • → Lactate >2 mmol/L with suspected infection meets Sepsis-3 septic shock criteria
  • → India accounts for 10–12 million sepsis cases annually — largely under-diagnosed
  • → ICU-acquired infections affect up to 30% of admitted patients — prevention is critical

💨 Ventilation Facts

  • → Ventilator-associated pneumonia (VAP) increases ICU stay by 8–10 days on average
  • → Prone positioning for 16+ hours/day reduces ARDS mortality by 16% (PROSEVA trial)
  • → Only 6 mL per kilogram of body weight — the lung-protective ventilation tidal volume target
  • → Daily spontaneous breathing trials (SBTs) help identify readiness for extubation

🌱 Post-ICU Facts

  • → PICS (Post-Intensive Care Syndrome) affects 30–50% of ICU survivors long-term
  • → ICU-acquired weakness (ICUAW) affects 25–50% of patients mechanically ventilated >7 days
  • → Early physiotherapy in the ICU (within 48 hours) dramatically reduces ICUAW incidence
  • → ICU diaries kept by nursing staff reduce PTSD in survivors by up to 35%
