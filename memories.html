A Personal Note

A Promise from Dr. Jay

Dear Families,

Every patient who enters my ICU becomes my responsibility — not just medically, but as a human being. I've learned that healing isn't just about ventilators and medications. It's about the hand you hold at 3 AM. The honest conversation that gives you peace. The call I make even when I'm not on duty.

To every family reading this — whether your loved one is in the ICU right now, or you're here remembering a journey you survived — know this: You were never alone, and you never will be.

Every story on this page is a testament to what's possible when medicine meets compassion, and when families never stop believing. You fought alongside your loved one every single day. That courage humbles me.

With gratitude for your trust,

Dr. Jay Kothari
Consultant, Critical Care Medicine · Apollo Hospitals, Ahmedabad
